Pharmacy Management system

Login Form

Pharmacy Management System is a web/mobile application to manage an online pharmacy store. The application helps pharmacists to sell drugs, consult the available stocks of drugs, add new products, and send orders to suppliers. this project was a teamwork and I worked on the Front-End part. The objective of the program is to help pharmacists to sell drugs, consult the available stocks of drugs, add new products and send orders to suppliers. The system must also make it possible to determine the money collected by the pharmacy over a given period. Ideally, the system should also allow staff to manage who is the pharmacist or the person who sold the drug. An alert mechanism for the management of best-selling products will be considered. You will be able to complete the functionalities according to your analysis in order to provide the best software on the market and strengthen your strategic position in a sector with strong market growth potential.
